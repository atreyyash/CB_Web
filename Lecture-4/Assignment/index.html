About Coding Blocks

We at Coding Blocks are on a mission to create skilled Software Engineers for our country and the world. We are here to bridge the gap between the quality of skills demanded by industry and the quality of skills imparted by conventional institutes. Programming is a lot of fun because, unlike other subjects, you get to instantly apply the concepts you are learning. At Coding Blocks, we thrive to increase student interest by providing hands on practical training on every concept taught in the classroom. We create confident developers who think beyond industrial jobs and rather march their ideas into self created entrepreneurship ventures. We also connect students to software companies via our placement assistance program.
